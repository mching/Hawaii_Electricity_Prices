Electricity Prices in Hawaii

  1. Prices for electricity in Hawaii are among the highest in the United States
  2. These prices vary not only by time but by island and sector
  3. Sectors include Residential, Commercial, and Street Light uses
  4. The following figure shows the data plotted with ggplot2 (Source: Hawaii Open Data)
